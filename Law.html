Constitutional Law Fundamentals):

Description:

Explore the foundational principles of constitutional law, a vital area of study in law school. Delve into the structure and powers of government, individual rights, and the balance of authority. Topics include judicial review, federalism, and the interpretation of constitutional provisions. Gain insights into landmark cases that have shaped constitutional jurisprudence.

This course equips students with a solid understanding of the U.S. Constitution and its impact on legal frameworks, fostering critical thinking and analytical skills essential for legal practice.
